This is

REBEL THREADS

We're an anti-corporate clothing brand that thrives on creative chaos, authentic rebellion, and community-driven design. Our pieces aren't just clothes — they're statements for the beautifully chaotic originals who refuse to fit into neat little boxes. If it challenges the norm, celebrates the weird, or makes corporate executives uncomfortable — we're into it.

Inside our rebellion / slightly organized chaos

We design clothes that rebel — against fast fashion, corporate bullshit, and the idea that you need to look like everyone else. From statement pieces to everyday chaos-wear, our collection lives in the space where authenticity meets quality, and conformity goes to die.

Every piece we create is a middle finger to mass production — where community feedback shapes designs, sustainability isn't just marketing speak, and clothes have stories worth telling. We like quality with an attitude, honest messaging that doesn't sugarcoat reality, and pieces that move like they own the place.

REBEL THREADS isn't just a brand name — it's a way of thinking. We've spent years building a community of creative misfits, sustainable fashion advocates, and people who believe clothes should reflect personality, not conformity. If that sounds like your vibe, welcome to the beautiful chaos.
